BART Data Analytics: Passenger Concentration vs. Time of Day/Station

Purpose:

The purpose of this study was to break down BART commute pattens and to represent these patterns informative visuals.

Research questions:

This study is intended to provide some insights based on the below guiding questions.

  • What is the "busiest" BART station?
  • What time of day is the "busiest" time to commute on BART?
  • When is the best time to commute into San Francisco?

    • Conclusions:

    The graphs provide information on passenger concentration for a sample two-week period (June 1-15) in 2016 based on station and time of day.

    Based on the data visualizations, we can conclude that commuting hours (7am-9am and 4pm-7pm) tend to the most "busy" times on BART. It is also evident in the map that the stations in the Financial District are the busiest stations of BART based on the number of people going in and out of BART. The busiest day per station varies between stations but weekdays tend to be more busy compared to weekends.
